Research

Research by PTCAT Research Consortium is funded through the Development Program for Convergence R&D over Traditional Culture and Current Technology by the National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF) grants funded by the Ministry of Education and by the Ministry of Science, ICT & Future Planning.

  • Soil-Based 3D Materials & Processing (Including Rapid Thermal Processing)
  • Wood-Based 3D Materials & Processing

  • Large-Scale 3D Printing Systems
  • Data Informatics for Traditional Culture and Advanced Technology

Some of these topics and others are highlighted in greater detail below


Soil-Based 3D Materials & Processing

PTCAT research in Hongik University includes developing 3D materials and processing based on soil-based materials and the new composites are attempted in the multiphase mixtures with the aim to achieving high-strength 3D materials hardening in a short time period



Advanced Illumination Optics for Rapid Hardening

Ever-increasing technology advancement in 3D printing demands the introduction of new optical illumination optical concepts which allow for ultra rapid thermal hardening in association with the accelerated chemical reactions. Such endeavors are attempted through flash-lamp annealing and/or VCSEL (Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Lasers). HU researchers are developing quantitative predictive models of thermal annealing along with the introduction of the actual prototypes.
