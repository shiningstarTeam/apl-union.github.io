Education

PTCAT students, postdocs, and faculty organize a regular reading group to discuss foundational material and the latest research in 3D printing area.  For more information, see the reading group homepage.
PTCAT faculty engage in undergraduate, graduate, and executive education on the topics of 3D printing.  Representative courses taught by PTCAT faculty and other courses related to PTCAT interests are listed below.  Several courses are taught on an experimental basis, assigned as regular course numbers.  Yearly course planners, and more information about the courses, can be found here.


Undergraduate Courses

  • Introduction to 3D Printing
  • Introduction to Measurement & Control
  • Introduction to 3D Modeling Using Fusion 360
  • 3D Printing Materials Laboratory
  • Statistical Analysis & Technology Management

Graduate Courses

  • 3D Printing: Principles & Applications
  • Measurement & Control: Data Analytics and Applications
  • Engineering Statistics
  • Complex Materials: Design & Applicaitons
